About.

A brief overview of this issue.

On Thursday, Feb. 24, 2022, troops from Russia crossed the border and launched an attack on Ukraine’s North, South, and East regions. The situation is quickly unfolding, with attacks on Ukraine having taken place continuously throughout the past few days, threatening the safety of millions of people in the country. In response, citizens around the world have shown their solidarity with Ukraine in various ways.

Whats at stake?

Why this issue is important.

Aggression will continue to result in violence, as hundreds of thousands of people are being displaced while others continue to fight for their rights of sovereignty.
